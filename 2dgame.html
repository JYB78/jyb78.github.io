Classic 2D Shooter Game

A simple and classic arcade game that I made using Unity and C# just for fun. It is a fixed 2D shooter game where the player's goal is to protect the space station behind your ship. The player will earn points by destroying enemy ships. The player can control their ship using the W/S keys and use the SPACE key to shoot bullets. Be careful, the amount and speed of enemy ships will increase over-time!
